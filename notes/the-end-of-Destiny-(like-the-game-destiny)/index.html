the end of Destiny (like, the game destiny)

X

so, today at 6pm bungie posted a proper sizeable update to the game for the first time in a long time,,,

destiny 2 is being "sunset" effectively, theyre stopping all live service updates after the next,,, i wanted to write out my feelings on it because as silly as it is the franchise has meant a lot to me
12 years, nearly 10,000 hours altogether in the franchise,,, so im obviously pretty sad about it but i wanna write how i feel more in depth

i think its a pretty bittersweet ending, we're getting a lot of cool stuff… practically everything the community could and has ever asked bungie for,,, ontop of the dlc content theyre adding srl, old raids through pantheon, traditional weekly content rotators, bunch of cool stuff and ontop of that the destinations page is also coming back… but all of that? its the end, its the last thing and it feels so odd to think about having grown up with the game

im 20 now, i was 8 when i first played the franchise in the summer of 2014, i saw someone from school was playing it and i wanted to find things to do with people from school so i played and i loved it

anddd ive pretty much played since then,,, very few breaks in between tbh, thats how the 10,000 number is a thing

hell the first piece of content i made for a game/in a game engine WAS destiny themed, it was the khvostov bonelab mod back in june-july 2024

im gonna miss the franchise, a lot, its grown as i have but… its the right time, maybe a little late even honestly… the final shape wouldve been a good place to end it i think, maybe the edge of fate but not renegades (or i guess whatever is coming from shadow and order, sure we dont know it could be a good place to stop but i doubt it with how theyd been talking about updates)

i loaded into the tower later that night,,, 2:15am full of people mourning the game talking in local chat, we talked, it was really nice,,,
